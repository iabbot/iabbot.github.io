What is the cII Privacy Policy? What data do you collect?

Back to cII

The cII privacy policy is as thus: I'm not going to sell your information. Your account information is stored in an encrypted database (bar your username).

iab collects the following information that can be tied back to your cII (and thus Discord) account:

  • The size(width, height), parameters, and associated user with any cII-associated AkaiCuda project.

The following information is stored in your cII account entry:

  • Your selected username.
  • Your password, not in plaintext (of course).
  • Your email, encrypted.
