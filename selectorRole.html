$ refers to a role to be targeted. A target can either be the exact name of the role, its position (with @everyone being 0 (although most commands do not allow @everyone to be selected)), its mention, or its id.

As an example, let's take a role named Members, with the position of 1 (second lowest role) and an id of 1291.

Valid ways to target this role are as follows

  • Members
  • @Members
  • 1
  • 1291

Invalid ways to target this user are as follows

  • members (the name must match exactly.)
